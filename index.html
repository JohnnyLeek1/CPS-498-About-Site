ABOUT

Super Intricate Gamified Market Analysis (SIGMA) is a gamified stock market simulation in the form of a modern web page which heavily emphasizes social interaction. Users can create a profile using either their email or social media accounts and can invite their friends to compete in various market competitions. Each competition lasts for a certain time frame (specified by the competition creator) and has different goals depending on the competition type. Users can customize an avatar using collectibles which are unlocked by participating in various tournaments and challenges, alongside collecting achievements by completing tasks during their competitions. While using the SIGMA site, users can learn more about the stock market and how they can intelligently invest their money. SIGMA asks about the userâ€™s general knowledge with investing and will tailor the learning experience to accommodate. SIGMA allows users to learn how to trade on the stock market without fear of losing actual money. The goal of SIGMA is to get users to feel confident in taking what they have learned from the game and applying it to the actual stock market while also keeping users coming back to play because they enjoy the game.
