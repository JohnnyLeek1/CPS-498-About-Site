So... what is it?

We've come all this way, and I just realized you might not even know what SIGMA is!

SIGMA was created as a way to introduce people to the stock market without the heaviest barrier to entry: having access to money. We decided the solution was to allow users to "paper trade". That is, make trades on the market and have a portfolio, but using fake money. In order to maintain realism, SIGMA follows the real stock market, providing accurate real-time data to its users. Not only this, we wanted to keep our people engaged and learning, and what better way than to "gameify" the system. As such, SIGMA incorporates social stock market competitions that users can compete in against their friends, or even just random strangers to see who can execute the best trades. Users can keep track of multiple portfolios to try out different strategies, keep a watchlist of stocks they're interested in, and look at their historical performance. SIGMA also features engaging video content showcasing different strategies and teaching users about stock market fundamentals.

To encourage participation and learning, we also have a fully-developed avatar system: Sigmojis! That's what I am! These avatars can be customized by the user by unlocking various cosmetics earned by participating in competitions, completing challenges, or by leveling up. Every item has a certain rarity attached to it, so users can work towards increasingly difficult challenges to unlock the most rare cosmetics for their Sigmoji!
